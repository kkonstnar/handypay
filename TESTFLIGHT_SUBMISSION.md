**Create External Testing Group:**

1. Go to **TestFlight** â†’ **External Testing**
2. Click **+** to create a new group
3. Name your group (e.g., "HandyPay Beta Testers")
4. Add testers by email address

1. Select your build
2. Click **Submit for Review**
